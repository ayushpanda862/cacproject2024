Administration

The administrative arm of Bucks County government coordinates a wide range of services, including emergency response, community and human services, the county prison system, voter registration, and elections.

Board of Elections

The Board of Elections is responsible for preparing and furnishing all materials used during elections, registering new voters, and certifying election results.

Row Officers

Each of the county's nine row officers is elected at large for a four-year term, overseeing functions such as property assessments and tax collection.

Department of Health

The Bucks County Department of Health handles vaccinations, restaurant inspections, and public health education programs to promote community wellness.

General Services

The General Services Division encompasses all operational departments, focusing on improving efficiency and delivering quality services to the public.

Human Resources

The Human Resources Department enhances the effectiveness of county operations by providing innovative services and critical workforce data.

Human Services

The Division of Human Services supports communities and strengthens individual and family wellness through collaboration and resource stewardship.

Planning Commission

The Bucks County Planning Commission guides development and land use planning to promote sustainable growth in the county.

Departments

Bucks County comprises various departments that provide a multitude of services to residents, working collaboratively to address community needs.

Community Resources

Bucks County offers a range of community resources including public libraries, recreational parks, and community centers. These facilities provide residents with access to educational programs, recreational activities, and community engagement opportunities, fostering a vibrant community spirit.

Learn More

To learn more about Bucks County, how it functions, and the services available, visit the official county website. You can also explore the various departments and their resources to stay informed about community events and programs.
