Upcoming Legislation

HB 2595

Short Title: This bill aims to create a program focused on developing a robust workforce for nursing assistants, addressing the growing demand in healthcare.

Actions: Currently under review by the Labor and Industry Committee, as of September 30, 2024.

HB 2597

Short Title: This legislation proposes changes to the Public School Code to incorporate health centers within schools, ensuring students have access to vital health services.

Actions: This bill has been sent to the Health Committee for consideration, as of September 30, 2024.

HB 2601

Short Title: This act focuses on legislative redistricting and aims to include incarcerated individuals in population counts, promoting fair representation.

Actions: Referred to the State Government Committee for further evaluation on September 30, 2024.

HB 2604

Short Title: This bill establishes the Emerging Psychologists Workforce Initiative, supporting the growth and training of future psychologists to meet community needs.

Actions: Currently with the Health Committee for review as of September 30, 2024.

HR 543

Short Title: This resolution proclaims October 2024 as "Liver Disease Awareness Month," aiming to raise awareness and educate the public about liver health.

Actions: Under consideration by the Health Committee since September 30, 2024.

HR 544

Short Title: This resolution recognizes November 11, 2024, as "Veterans Day" in Pennsylvania, honoring the service and sacrifices of veterans.

Actions: Currently in the Veterans Affairs and Emergency Preparedness Committee for review, as of September 30, 2024.

HR 545

Short Title: This resolution acknowledges October 9, 2024, as "Ageism Awareness Day" in Pennsylvania, encouraging discussions about ageism and promoting respect for older adults.

Actions: Referred to the Aging and Older Adult Services Committee for consideration since September 30, 2024.
