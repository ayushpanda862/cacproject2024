Voting Resources in Pennsylvania

Voter Registration

To register to vote in Pennsylvania, visit the Pennsylvania Bureau of Elections's website. You can register online, by mail, or in person. The registration deadline is 15 days before an election.

Voting Methods

In Pennsylvania, you can vote in person on Election Day, during early voting, or by mail-in ballot. Find detailed information on these options at VotePA.

Voter ID Requirements

When voting in Pennsylvania, you must show a form of ID if you are voting for the first time. Acceptable IDs include a driver's license, state ID, or a passport. More information can be found on VotePA.

Election Protection

If you experience issues while voting, contact the Election Protection Hotline at 1-866-OUR-VOTE for assistance, or reach out to the Pennsylvania Bureau of Elections.

Voter Education Resources

Stay informed about Pennsylvania's voting process and issues through organizations and local civic groups.

Get Involved

Learn how you can volunteer for voter registration drives, assist others with the voting process, or become a poll worker in your community. Visit VotePA for more information.
